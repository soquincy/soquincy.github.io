Definitions
Throughout this policy:

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, why it is collected, how it is stored, and who it may be shared with for Freesona-based bot instances operated by Quincy So.

By using the bot, you agree to the collection and use of data as described in this Privacy Policy.

Project Scope
This bot is based on the public open-source project Freesona. This Privacy Policy applies only to Freesona-based bot instances operated by Quincy So.

Because Freesona is publicly available, anyone may host their own independent instance with different configurations, logging practices, memory systems, hosting providers, or privacy policies.

If you are interacting with a Freesona-based bot that is not operated by Quincy So, this Privacy Policy does not apply to that instance.
