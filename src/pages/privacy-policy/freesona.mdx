## 1. Information We Collect

When you use a Freesona-based bot instance operated by Quincy So, we may collect basic interaction data such as messages sent to the bot, command usage, and server identifiers needed to provide the service. We may also collect diagnostic or error information to improve reliability.

## 2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used to operate the bot, respond to requests, enforce rules, debug issues, and improve performance. We do not sell personal data.

## 3. Data Sharing

We may share limited data with third-party AI, hosting, or processing providers that are necessary to fulfill your requests. Those providers are subject to their own privacy terms.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for operation, security, or legal obligations. Where applicable, you may request deletion of your data through the designated support channels.

## 5. Your Choices

You may avoid sharing unnecessary information by not using features that require it. Server administrators may also apply their own moderation policies.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the bot means you accept any changes to this policy.

## 7. Contact

For questions or privacy-related requests, contact the operator through the designated support channels.
